MOSPI, the Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation, is a significant institution in India dedicated to the collection, analysis, and dissemination of crucial statistical data. Since its establishment in 1999, MOSPI has played a vital role in providing accurate and timely information related to various socio-economic aspects of the country. Through its data collection and analysis efforts, MOSPI supports evidence-based decision-making, aids in policy formulation, and assists in the planning and implementation of development initiatives. The ministry's role in ensuring the availability of reliable statistics contributes to better governance and fosters sustainable growth in the nation.
